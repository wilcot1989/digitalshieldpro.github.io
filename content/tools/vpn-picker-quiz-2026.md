## When this quiz won't help you

The quiz assumes you actually want a VPN. There are situations where you don't:

- **Just torrenting on home network**: Your ISP doesn't care about most P2P traffic. A VPN adds latency. If you're worried about copyright notices, yes, get a VPN. Otherwise, skip.
- **"Hide from the government"**: A VPN protects against passive surveillance and most ISP tracking. It does NOT protect against a targeted state-level adversary. For that you need Tor + operational security training.
- **"Hide from work / school"**: Most workplaces ban VPN use. Doing this from a work-managed device usually triggers DLP / network monitoring alerts. Use personal device + cellular hotspot if necessary.
- **"Get free streaming forever"**: VPN-Netflix unblocking works for a few months until Netflix updates IP detection. Plan for periodic rotation.

## After you pick a VPN — setup checklist

Whatever VPN you end up with, these are non-negotiable settings:

1. **Always-on**: Connect at boot. Don't rely on remembering to turn it on.
2. **Kill switch enabled**: If VPN drops, all internet drops. Otherwise IP leaks during reconnect.
3. **DNS leak protection on**: Use VPN's DNS or Cloudflare 1.1.1.1, not ISP's.
4. **WireGuard protocol** when available — faster than OpenVPN, modern crypto.
5. **2FA on the VPN account itself** — TOTP authenticator app, not SMS.
