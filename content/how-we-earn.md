## How Digital Shield Pro Makes Money

Short answer: **affiliate commissions**. Long answer below.

### No advertising
You'll see no Google AdSense, third-party banners, sponsored content or pop-ups. That's a deliberate choice — ads distract, slow the site, and give Google data about you. We want none of those three.

### Yes: affiliate commissions
When you buy something via a product link on our site, the vendor pays us a fee. Concrete examples:

| Product type | Commission | Example |
|--------------|------------|---------|
| VPN | 30-45% of first year | NordVPN |
| Password mgr | 30-40% recurring | NordPass |
| Encrypted email | $5-$15 per signup | ProtonMail |
| Antivirus | 20-30% per signup | Bitdefender |

Monthly this earns enough to cover:
- Hosting + domain + CDN (~€30/mo)
- Tools (analytics, writing, AI assistance, ~€100/mo)
- Time for research + writing + updating (variable)

### What this means for you
1. **Our content is free** — no paywall, no subscription
2. **Our reviews stay honest** — we get the same commission whether we recommend a product or not, so we have no incentive to lie
3. **No ads means a faster site** and no tracking junk

### How we choose which products to cover
We only cover a product if:
- It's actually relevant for our audience
- We have enough information for an honest assessment
- It falls within our domain expertise (we don't write about crypto trading bots, gambling, or dropshipping courses)

### How we choose what to RECOMMEND
A higher commission gets NO priority. Our top pick is always the product that:
1. Works best for the most typical reader
2. Offers good price/performance
3. Has a company behind it that we trust for the long term
4. Has no dark patterns (lock-in, cancellation hurdles, dark UI)

Sometimes our top pick is a product with LOWER commission. That's fine.
