## What Should a Security Suite Include?

Before comparing specific products, here is what a comprehensive security suite should offer:

### Essential Components
- **Antivirus and anti-malware** — Real-time protection against viruses, trojans, ransomware, spyware, and zero-day threats
- **Firewall** — Network traffic monitoring beyond what the Windows Firewall provides
- **VPN** — Encrypted internet connection for privacy on public Wi-Fi and general browsing
- **Password manager** — Secure storage for all your passwords with auto-fill

### Valuable Extras
- **Identity theft protection** — Dark web monitoring, data breach alerts, credit monitoring
- **Parental controls** — Content filtering, screen time limits, activity monitoring
- **Cloud backup** — Encrypted storage for important files
- **Webcam and microphone protection** — Alerts when apps access your camera
- **Secure browser** — Hardened browser for banking and shopping
- **Device optimization** — Cleanup tools and performance monitoring

The best suites cover all of the essentials and most of the extras. For standalone antivirus comparisons, see our [best antivirus software guide](/posts/best-antivirus-software-2026/).
