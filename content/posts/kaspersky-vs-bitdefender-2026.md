## Features Comparison

### Antivirus and Anti-Malware

Both include real-time scanning, scheduled scans, quick scans, full system scans, custom scans, USB drive scanning, and boot-time scanning. Bitdefender adds a Rescue Environment that can scan and clean an infected system before Windows starts, which is useful for removing persistent rootkits.

### Ransomware Protection

**Bitdefender** offers multi-layered ransomware protection including behavioral detection, ransomware remediation (automatically restoring encrypted files from protected backups), and Safe Files that prevents unauthorized access to designated folders.

**Kaspersky** provides System Watcher behavioral analysis that detects ransomware patterns and can roll back malicious file changes. It also includes Kaspersky's anti-ransomware tool that monitors for suspicious encryption activity.

Both are highly effective against ransomware. Bitdefender's automatic remediation and Safe Files feature give it a slight edge.

### Web Protection

Both block malicious websites, phishing pages, and dangerous downloads. Bitdefender includes TrafficLight, a browser extension that rates search results for safety. Kaspersky includes Safe Messaging that scans links in messaging apps.

### VPN

**Kaspersky Premium** includes an unlimited VPN with no data caps, servers in 70+ countries, and support for streaming and torrenting. The VPN is powered by Kaspersky's own infrastructure and provides decent speeds.

**Bitdefender** includes a VPN with a 200 MB per day data cap on Total Security and lower plans. Unlimited VPN is only available on Bitdefender Premium Security, which costs significantly more. The VPN uses Hotspot Shield's infrastructure.

For a full-featured VPN experience, neither matches dedicated VPN services like [NordVPN](/posts/nordvpn-review-2026/) or [Surfshark](/posts/surfshark-review-2026/), but Kaspersky offers far more VPN value at the standard price tier. See our [best VPN services guide](/posts/best-vpn-services-2026/) for dedicated VPN recommendations.

**Winner: Kaspersky** wins decisively on VPN value.

### Banking and Payment Protection

**Kaspersky Safe Money** is one of the best banking protection features available. It opens financial websites in a hardened, isolated browser session that is separated from the rest of your system. It automatically activates when you visit banking sites and prevents keyloggers, screen capture malware, and clipboard hijacking from stealing your credentials.

**Bitdefender Safepay** offers a similar secured browser for online banking and shopping. It is functional and secure but slightly less polished than Kaspersky's implementation. Safepay does not automatically detect banking sites as reliably as Safe Money.

**Winner: Kaspersky.** Safe Money is the gold standard for banking protection in consumer antivirus.

### Password Manager

**Bitdefender** includes a password manager in all its plans, though with limited entries (some tiers cap at 50 passwords). The unlimited password manager is included in Premium Security.

**Kaspersky** includes Kaspersky Password Manager with its Premium plan, offering unlimited password storage, secure notes, and document storage.

Both provide basic but functional password management. For a more capable password manager, consider a dedicated solution. ## The US Ban: What It Means for Kaspersky

In 2024, the US government prohibited the sale of Kaspersky products to new customers within the United States, citing concerns about potential influence from the Russian government. This is arguably the most significant factor differentiating these two products.

### What Happened

The US Department of Commerce issued a Final Determination prohibiting Kaspersky Lab and its affiliates from providing antivirus software and cybersecurity products or services in the United States. The ban was based on concerns that the Russian government could compel Kaspersky to use its software for espionage or cyber attacks.

### Impact on Users

- **US residents** can no longer purchase new Kaspersky subscriptions
- **Existing US customers** were given a transition period to switch to alternative products
- **Users outside the US** are unaffected and can continue to purchase and use Kaspersky normally
- Kaspersky's software itself has not been shown to contain any malicious code or backdoors
- Independent labs continue to test and certify Kaspersky's products

### What This Means for Your Decision

If you are in the United States, the choice is made for you: Bitdefender is the clear winner since Kaspersky is simply not available. If you are outside the US, the ban does not directly affect you, but you may want to consider the trust implications. Some users feel uncomfortable with the geopolitical uncertainty, while others note that independent testing and transparency audits have found no evidence of wrongdoing.

**Our recommendation:** If you have any concerns about trust or data handling, choose Bitdefender. If you are outside the US and comfortable with Kaspersky's transparency measures, it remains an excellent antivirus product.
