## Location history

Google Timeline (Maps) records every place your phone has been. Disable it: Maps then Settings then Location settings then Timeline off. Then delete the history. Do the same for Apple Significant Locations: Settings then Privacy and Security then Location Services then System Services then Significant Locations then Clear.
