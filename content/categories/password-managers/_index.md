The average person has 100+ online accounts. A password manager generates unique, strong passwords for every account and remembers them for you. It's the single most impactful security tool you can use.

## Our Top Picks

- **[1Password](/posts/best-password-managers-2026/)** — Best overall. Excellent interface, Watchtower breach monitoring, Travel Mode.
- **[Bitwarden](/posts/best-password-managers-2026/)** — Best free option. Unlimited passwords on unlimited devices.
- **[NordPass](/posts/best-password-managers-2026/)** — Most affordable premium option. Modern XChaCha20 encryption.
