## Contact

Have a question, feedback or suggestion? Email **[contact@digitalshieldpro.com](mailto:contact@digitalshieldpro.com)** — we read every message and usually respond within 48 hours.

### When to email
- **Factual errors** in an article? Please tell us — corrections take priority
- **Personal experience with a product** to share? Welcome — we update articles based on reader feedback
- **Comparison request** for products not yet on the site? Suggestions go on our planning queue

### What NOT to email about
- Personal advice that requires a licensed professional (financial, legal, medical). Get a specialist.
- Purchase support for products — that's the vendor's job
- Sponsorship / guest-post offers will not be answered

### Postal address
Digital Shield Pro is an online publication. No physical office. For formal correspondence: email above.
